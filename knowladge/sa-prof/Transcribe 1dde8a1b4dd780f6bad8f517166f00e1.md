# Transcribe

## **Amazon Transcribe - Key Concepts**

Amazon Transcribe is an AWS service that automatically converts speech into text.**1** It utilizes a deep learning process called Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) for rapid and accurate transcription.**2**

## **Key Features**

- **PII Redaction:** Automatically identifies and removes Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from transcriptions (e.g., age, name, Social Security Number).
- **Automatic Language Identification:** Detects and transcribes audio in multiple languages within the same audio stream (e.g., English, French, Spanish).

## **Use Cases**

Amazon Transcribe can be used for:

- **Transcribing Customer Service Calls:** Analyzing call content for insights and quality assurance.
- **Automating Closed Captioning and Subtitling:** Generating text for video accessibility.
- **Generating Metadata for Media Assets:** Creating searchable archives of audio and video content.

## **Demonstration Highlights**

- **Real-time Transcription:** Audio input is transcribed into text almost instantaneously.
- **PII Removal:** The service can automatically identify and redact sensitive information like names and phone numbers.
- **Multilingual Transcription:** Transcribe can recognize and transcribe audio in multiple languages within a single stream.
